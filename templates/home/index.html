About Garden Planner

Our platform helps you plan, manage, and optimize your home garden. Get personalized gardening tips, track plant growth, and receive reminders for essential gardening tasks. Join our community forum to share your gardening experiences and learn from others.

Featured Tips

  • Water your plants early in the morning to reduce water loss due to evaporation.
  • Use mulch to help retain soil moisture and control weeds.
  • Rotate your crops to prevent soil depletion and pest buildup.
